## Users

Potential players discovering the game for the first time via a shared link or ad. They land with no prior context and decide in seconds whether to keep exploring or bounce. No secondary audience confirmed yet.

## Product Purpose

A landing page for a game project (f5game). It introduces the game and drives visitors toward playing it. Success looks like a visitor clicking through to play rather than leaving.

## Brand Personality

Playful, bright, and fun. The page should feel energetic and inviting rather than serious or corporate — it's the front door to a game, not a business tool.

## Anti-references

Avoid the generic, templated look of AI-generated landing pages: predictable gradient-glow hero text, stock pill badges, and interchangeable SaaS-marketing layout. The page should feel distinct and crafted, not assembled from a template.

## Design Principles

- Playfulness first: energy and fun should read immediately, not after scrolling.
- Distinct over derivative: actively resist patterns that read as generic AI-generated marketing.
- One clear next step: the "play" action stays the obvious, unmissable move on the page.
